Are you leaning toward a specialty in medicine? Whether you want to practice family medicine or psychiatry, OSU Center for Health Sciences and the Osteopathic Medical Education Consortium of Oklahoma offer multiple accredited residency and fellowship positions across Oklahoma.

Residency and Fellowship Facts

  • OSU-CHS offers 200+ accredited residency and fellowship positions.
  • OMECO, staffed by OSU-CHS Office of Graduate Medical Education, offers 184 accredited residency positions.
  • In total, OSU and OMECO provide 461 residency positions in clinics and hospitals throughout Oklahoma.
  • About 100 new residency positions will be added with the opening of the Veterans Hospital in Tulsa and the Oklahoma Psychiatric Care Center in the OSU Academic Medical District in 2026.
